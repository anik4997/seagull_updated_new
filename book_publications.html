Submission Process

Once you are ready to submit, Seagull offers two types of submission process. Online Submission and Email Submission. To proceed with the online submission please fill Online Book Submission Proposal . If you want to submit Book Publication Proposal via Email Submission please Download the MS document Form Book Proposal Form Download and fill out the form and send it to our book proposal Editor Mailbox mentioning Book Proposal in (Your Working Title- e.g. Business Economics) in the subject line of your email.

Publish Article with Seagull Journals

This content will help you to publish journal articles with Seagull Journals. To proceed publishing journal article with us, kindly follow the steps below.

Find Journal

Find your desired journal by visiting our Journal List page.

Submit Manuscript

You will have found two Submission options from the journal website. One is Online Submission System and another one is Email Submission. You can prefer any of these. We accept all journals manuscript to our Editor Mailbox. Kindly mention Submission for (Journal abbreviated name) in the subject line of your mailbox and send it to our Editor Mailbox

Track Submission

Once you have successfully submitted a paper to your desired journal. The system will provide a Manuscript ID instantly. You will also be notified about your paper status via your registered Email. Track Submission inputting the Manuscript ID in that form

Stay Connected

Publishing papers sometimes take about one or more than one month due to the Reviewing, Editing and commenting. During this time journal editor can contact you via Email of Phone. So, we expect your cooperation and excellent communication with our journal editor. Your good connection with the journal will advance the publication process effectively.

Promote Your Publication

Once your paper published finally. You can download your paper via website or your Dashboard. You can promote your paper by sharing, distributing without business purpose. We suggest you to add your published paper in your Institution Digital Library, and your research profile such as academia.edu, researchgate.net, orcid.org and somewhere else.

Publishing Ethic

The publication of an article in a peer-reviewed journal is an essential building block in the development of a coherent and respected network of knowledge. It is a direct reflection of the quality of the work of the authors and the institutions that support them. Peer-reviewed articles support and embody the scientific method. It is therefore important to agree upon standards of expected ethical behavior for all parties involved in the act of publishing: the author, the journal editor, the peer reviewer, the publisher and the society of society-owned or sponsored journals. Seagull is partnered with COPE (Committee on Publication Ethics), to learn more about publishing ethics of various person's role and responsibilities of an article publication to any of a journal please visit COPE Website
